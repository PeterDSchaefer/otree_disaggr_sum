Revenue depends on the market size, market share, and sales price:

Revenue = Market size × Market share × Sales price.

Both you and the owner know that these three parameters lie within certain ranges, over which they are uniformly distributed. That is, each value within the range is equally likely to occur:

1. Market size (units): 25, 26, ..., 35;

2. Market share (%): 20, 21, ..., 40;

3. Sales price (ECU per unit): 20, 21, ..., 30.

Taken together, both you and the owner know that the revenue lies between 100 and 420 ECU and that its probability distribution looks as shown below.
